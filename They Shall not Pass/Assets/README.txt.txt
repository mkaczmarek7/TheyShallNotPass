Project name: They Shall Not Pass
Programmer: Mateusz Kaczmarek

Info:
-TSNP is the mixed of point click mode and tower defence 
-The primary goal is to survive waves of dinosaurs
-4 kind of enemies
-3 power ups: 
*frozen power - stops enemies for a few seconds
*asteroid power - after clicking the button user choose place to set the target of respawned asteroid
*carnige power - create 3 cannons which shoot to dinosaurs

Good luck & have fun!
